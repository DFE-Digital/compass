# Epic: Product Governance (RAID) - Risks

## Description
This epic focuses on identifying and managing risks related to product governance.

## User Story
As a product manager, I want to ensure all potential risks are identified and managed systematically, so that we can mitigate their impacts on product delivery.

## Checklist
- [ ] Identify risk categories
- [ ] Document risks in the system
- [ ] Review risks regularly

## Acceptance Criteria
- Risks are categorized and accessible in the risk management system.
- All identified risks can be linked to specific products.
